103rd Mangaluru Sarvajanika Shree Sharada Mahotsava 2025

Schedule of Events and Programs

Grand Arrival

September 28, 2025 • 8:00 PM

Join us for the grand arrival of Shri Sharada Matha from SVT Rajangan at 8 PM on September 28,2025. The arrival procession will commence from Rajangan, proceeding via Umamaheshwar Temple Road, Ram Mandir, Nandadeep Road, Flower Market Cross Road, and Flower Market, culminating at Acharya Mutt Vasantha Mantap.

Vigraha Prathista

September 29, 2025 • 6:00 AM
नमस्ते शारदा देवी काश्मीरपुरवासिनि ।
त्वामहं प्रार्थये नित्यं विद्यादानं च देहि मे ॥

Shree Sharada Matha's Vigraha Prathishta Pooja begins at 6:00 AM on September 29, 2025.

Durga Namaskar

September 29, 2025 • 8:00 PM
सर्वमङ्गलमाङ्गल्ये शिवे सर्वार्थसाधिके।
शरण्ये त्र्यम्बके गौरि नारायणि नमोऽस्तु ते॥

Durga Namaskara Seva at 8:00 PM on September 29, 2025.

Vidyarambha Seva

September 29, 2025 • 11:00 AM
सरस्वति नमस्तुभ्यं वरदे कामरूपिणि ।
विद्यारम्भं करिष्यामि सिद्धिर्भवतु मे सदा॥

Salutations to you, O Saraswati, the giver of all boons and the fulfiller of desires. I am beginning my studies; may I always have success.

Vidyarambha Seva at 11:00 AM on September 29, 2025.

Vishesha Deepalankar

October 2, 2025 • 6:00 PM onwards
दीपज्योतिः परब्रह्म दीपज्योतिः जनार्दनः ।
दीपेना हरतु पापं संध्यादिपो नमोऽस्तु ते ॥

Vishesha Deepalankar in the evenings from 6:00 PM onwards on October 2, 2025.

Visarjan Pooja

October 3, 2025 • 5:00 AM

Visarjan Pooja of Shri Sharada Matha at 5:00 AM on October 3, 2025.

Visarjan Shobhayatra

October 3, 2025 • 8:00 PM

Attend the grand Visarjana Shobhayatra on October 3, 2025, at 8:00 PM, proceeding across Car Street.

Cultural Programs

September 29 - October 2, 2025 • 7:00 PM - 9:00 PM

Vibrant cultural programs take place daily from 7:00 PM to 9:00 PM at Saraswathi Kalamantapa, September 29 to October 2, 2025.
